Currency (CAD)
The move in the Canadian dollar against the holding's listing currency, weighted by exposure.
Macro

Commodity pressure
How the commodity complex relevant to the holding (energy, metals, grains) has moved over the recent window.
Macro
08
Sector rotation
How the holding's sector has performed against the broader market over the recent window.
Macro
09
News tone shift
The change in tone across stories mentioning the holding, over a rolling window.
News
10
Event volume
The number of distinct news events mentioning the holding in the recent window, normalised to baseline.

Story direction
Whether the dominant narrative has shifted (favourable ↔ unfavourable), tracked across stories.
News
12
News vs. macro alignment
Whether news tone and macro signals point the same way — agreement reinforces, contradiction softens.

On glyphs and colour. Every factor gets a distinct colour and a distinct glyph shape, so the legend reads under colour-blindness. The 12-factor palette is separate from the 5-band palette — the colours above identify which factor; the Quiet / Light / Active / Strong / Intense bands identify how much attention. The full specification lives in pulse-legend-spec.md.
