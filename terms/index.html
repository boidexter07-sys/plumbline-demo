What Plumbline is

Plumbline is a read-only analytical surface. The product shows you information about investments you already hold, in accounts you hold elsewhere, and gives you a read on the conditions around those holdings. Plumbline is a Canadian service. The asset universe includes Canadian-listed securities (TSX and TSXV), U.S. names (including those interlisted on TSX in CAD), selected cryptocurrencies, and selected commodities.

Plumbline is not a brokerage. Plumbline does not execute trades. Plumbline does not hold your money, does not hold your securities, does not custody your assets, and does not place orders on your behalf. Nothing on the product should be read as a solicitation to buy, sell, hold, or trim any specific security.

The product is built around three surfaces — your portfolio view, a sandbox for paper portfolios, and a macro and geopolitical context layer — all of which are observational. The product is not built to replace the work of a financial advisor, accountant, or tax professional, and it is not a substitute for any of them.

Plumbline is not registered with the Ontario Securities Commission (OSC), the Canadian Securities Administrators (CSA), or the Canadian Investment Regulatory Organization (CIRO) as a dealer, adviser, or trading venue, because Plumbline does not and cannot execute trades or provide personalized advice.
